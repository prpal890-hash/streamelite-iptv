Installation Guides

Follow these simple steps to configure your premium IPTV subscription on your preferred device.

🔥

Amazon Firestick / Fire TV

01

Enable Unknown Sources

Go to Settings > My Fire TV > Developer Options. Turn ON 'Apps from Unknown Sources'.

02

Install Downloader

Search for 'Downloader' in the Amazon App Store. Install and open it.

03

Download IPTV Smarters Pro

Enter the URL provided in your subscription email into the Downloader app and install the APK.

04

Login & Stream

Open Smarters Pro, select 'Login with Xtream Codes API', and enter your Username, Password, and Server URL.

📺

Samsung & LG Smart TV

01

Install App

Open your TV's App Store and search for 'IBO Player' or 'Smart IPTV (SIPTV)'. Install the app.

02

Get Device ID

Open the app. The screen will display your TV's unique MAC Address or Device ID. Note this down.

03

Send to Support

Provide your MAC Address to our WhatsApp support team or use our portal to upload your M3U link automatically.
